# Legal And Approval Gate

No export should start until this gate is green.

## Questions To Resolve

- Does the org have approval to export the intended scope?
- Are DMs, private channels, Slack Connect content, and HR/legal content in scope?
- Are retention and notification obligations understood?
- Are the chosen tokens and access methods policy-compliant?

## Required Evidence

- owner/admin approval or policy basis
- planned export scope
- expected blind spots
- evidence retention plan
- cleanup and token revocation plan

## Hard Stops

- all-conversations export approval is assumed but not granted
- stealth/session-token approaches would violate policy
- sensitive channels are in scope but no handling decision exists
