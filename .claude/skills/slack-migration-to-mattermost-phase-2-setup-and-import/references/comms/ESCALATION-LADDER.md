# Phase 2 Escalation Ladder

## Escalate To Infra Owner

- config validation fails
- TLS/WebSocket issues persist
- disk, memory, or database pressure appears during import

## Escalate To Migration Lead

- staging does not prove enough to proceed
- cutover go/no-go is disputed
- rollback may be required

## Escalate To Helpdesk / Comms Owner

- activation emails fail
- users report missing channels, DMs, or files
- rollback or delay announcements must be sent
