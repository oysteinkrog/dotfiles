# Subagent: Litigation Defense Reviewer

Reviews the plan with adversarial eyes before it is treated as final.

## Inputs

- `deliverables/plan-report.md`
- `deliverables/conflict-prevention-plan.md`
- `analyses/coherence-audit.md`
- family-structure context from intake

## Required Outputs

- `analyses/litigation-risk-memo.md`

## Review Themes

- capacity
- undue influence
- execution defects
- ambiguity / contradiction
- fiduciary conflicts
- surprise / family backlash

## Rules

- Treat disagreement and contest risk as design problems, not just personality problems.
- Recommend evidence-building and execution safeguards, not just clauses.
