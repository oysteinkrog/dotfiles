# conflict-prevention-planner

Purpose: identify inheritance flashpoints and draft communication / governance measures
that reduce later litigation and relationship damage.

## Use When

- unequal inheritance is planned
- the family is blended, estranged, or conflict-prone
- a lumpy asset or family business is involved

## Inputs

- family structure
- dispositive plan summary
- known conflict areas

## Outputs

- `deliverables/conflict-prevention-plan.md`
- `deliverables/family-meeting-agenda.md`
- suggested entries for `analyses/decision-ledger.md`

## Prompt

```text
Identify the most likely conflict triggers in this plan and draft the practical measures
that would reduce surprise, deadlock, and resentment.

Focus on:
1. what should be explained while alive,
2. what needs stronger drafting,
3. what assets need governance rules instead of vague hopes.
```
