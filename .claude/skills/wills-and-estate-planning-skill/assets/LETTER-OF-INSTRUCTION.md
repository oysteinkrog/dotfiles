## Things You Should Do Right Away

For executor / spouse / closest family:

1. Get death certificate copies (10-15 — needed for many institutions)
2. Notify Social Security (often funeral home does this)
3. Notify the three credit bureaus to flag account against ghost fraud
4. Locate this letter and the will
5. Contact the attorney listed above
6. Pets — feed and arrange care
7. Refrigerator — clear perishables
8. Set up controlled mail handling (forwarding, hold, or monitored access) for the executor
9. House — secure, change locks if estranged relatives might access
10. Vehicle keys gathered and secured
11. Take photos of jewelry, art, valuable items
12. Don't distribute anything yet — wait for attorney guidance
