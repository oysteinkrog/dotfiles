# Expatriate Abroad

A U.S. citizen or green-card holder living abroad can have domicile, foreign-asset, and local-law complications that overwhelm a domestic-only plan.

## Core Issues

- domicile and governing law
- local forced-heirship or probate system
- foreign real estate and accounts
- practical executor / trustee geography

## Planning Moves

- route to foreign-assets and domicile references
- confirm local counsel is needed for situs-country assets
- ensure U.S. and local documents do not contradict each other
