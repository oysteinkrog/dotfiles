# Polyamorous or Unconventional Family

When the lived family structure diverges from legal-default relationships, the plan has to compensate explicitly.

## Core Issues

- legal spouse vs partner reality
- housing and support expectations across multiple adults
- childcare and dependency realities not reflected in default inheritance law

## Planning Moves

- use explicit beneficiary, fiduciary, and occupancy provisions
- document the user's actual support priorities in plain language
- flag any area where state law cannot fully mirror the desired arrangement
