# Religious Minority

Religious practices may materially affect funeral, burial, inheritance timing, fiduciary selection, and charitable structuring.

## Core Issues

- disposition-of-remains timing and ritual requirements
- religious-charity preferences
- faith-specific family expectations around fairness or stewardship

## Planning Moves

- capture requirements in disposition and letter-of-instruction deliverables
- identify which customs are legally binding, operational, or purely aspirational
