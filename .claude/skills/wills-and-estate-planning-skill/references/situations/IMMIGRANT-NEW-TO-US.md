# Immigrant New to the U.S.

A newly U.S.-connected family may carry foreign-law assumptions, foreign assets, and noncitizen family structures that require explicit translation into U.S. planning.

## Core Issues

- noncitizen spouse or heirs
- foreign accounts or real estate
- mismatched naming conventions / legal names
- inherited assumptions about who controls family assets

## Planning Moves

- verify exact legal names and status
- route to foreign-assets and non-citizen spouse references where relevant
- avoid assuming foreign documents translate cleanly into U.S. institutions
