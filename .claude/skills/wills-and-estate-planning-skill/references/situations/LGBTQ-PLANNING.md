# LGBTQ Planning

Formal legal equality does not eliminate family-resistance, documentation, and practical-intake issues.

## Core Issues

- chosen family vs legal default family
- parentage and nonbiological-child recognition
- estranged next-of-kin risk
- healthcare and funeral decision-making if documents are weak

## Planning Moves

- use explicit nominations and authorizations
- confirm parentage / adoption / donor-conception facts
- prioritize incapacity and disposition-of-remains documents
