# Military

Military families often move frequently and rely on federal benefits that need beneficiary coordination.

## Load This File When

- the user is active duty, reserve, retired military, or receives survivor / service-related benefits

## Special Planning Issues

- repeated interstate moves
- SGLI / beneficiary coordination
- survivor benefits and pension elections
- deployment and emergency authority planning

## Documents To Collect

- benefit election records
- SGLI and retirement-benefit statements
- DD-214 or service records when relevant
- current POA / deployment-related authority documents

## Common Mistakes

- failing to refresh state-sensitive documents after frequent moves
- assuming military benefits automatically line up with the estate plan
