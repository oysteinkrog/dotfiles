# Real Estate Investor

Real-estate-heavy estates are title-driven, debt-sensitive, and often multi-state.

## Load This File When

- the user owns multiple rental, development, or vacation properties
- LLC structures, 1031 history, or family real-estate holdings matter

## Special Planning Issues

- title and entity structure by property
- ancillary probate and situs issues
- debt, guarantees, and liquidity at death
- family governance for shared-use property

## Documents To Collect

- deeds and mortgage statements
- LLC / partnership agreements
- insurance declarations
- 1031 and depreciation records if relevant

## Common Mistakes

- leaving multiple parcels to multiple heirs without governance
- forgetting to fund a trust with deed transfers
- underestimating debt and admin friction on death
