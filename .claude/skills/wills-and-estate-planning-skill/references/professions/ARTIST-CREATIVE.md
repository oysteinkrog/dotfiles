# Artist or Creative

Creators often own assets that keep paying long after death.

## Load This File When

- the user earns from royalties, licensing, copyrights, trademarks, or catalog income

## Special Planning Issues

- IP ownership and chain of title
- royalty and platform payment continuity
- moral-rights / reputation stewardship questions
- fragmented side-entity and contract structures

## Documents To Collect

- publishing / licensing agreements
- royalty statements
- copyright / trademark registrations
- platform and payment-account records

## Common Mistakes

- treating IP as sentimental property instead of an income-producing asset
- leaving rights to multiple heirs without management authority
