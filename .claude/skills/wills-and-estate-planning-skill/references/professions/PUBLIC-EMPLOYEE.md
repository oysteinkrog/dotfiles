# Public Employee

Public employees often have pension systems and death benefits that sit outside the main will / trust conversation.

## Load This File When

- the user has a government pension, deferred comp plan, or statutory survivor benefit regime

## Special Planning Issues

- pension beneficiary / survivor elections
- deferred-comp coordination
- life and disability benefits
- job-specific death benefits for police, fire, or similar roles

## Documents To Collect

- pension statements
- beneficiary designation records
- deferred-comp documents
- union or employer death-benefit summaries

## Common Mistakes

- spending all the planning energy on the will while leaving pension elections untouched
- assuming a spouse is protected when the election form says otherwise
