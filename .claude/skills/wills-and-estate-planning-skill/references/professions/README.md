# Profession-Specific Estate Planning Overlays

Different professions present distinct estate planning challenges — liability exposure, compensation structures, asset types, succession questions. These overlays supplement the tier-appropriate planning.

## Files in This Directory

- **PHYSICIAN.md** — malpractice liability, practice succession, disability, specific compensation structures
- **EXECUTIVE.md** — concentrated employer stock, RSUs, ISOs, NQSOs, deferred comp, non-compete implications
- **FOUNDER.md** — pre-IPO and post-IPO planning, QSBS, founder stock vesting, early liquidity
- **ATTORNEY.md** — malpractice exposure, partnership interests, IOLTA, succession
- **REAL-ESTATE-INVESTOR.md** — LLCs, 1031 exchanges, cost basis, refinancing at death
- **SOFTWARE-ENGINEER.md** — tech compensation (RSU, 401(k), ESPP), crypto, IP, concentrated positions
- **BUSINESS-OWNER.md** — valuation, buy-sell, succession, family employment policies
- **ARTIST-CREATIVE.md** — royalties, IP, copyrights, ongoing revenue streams
- **ATHLETE-ENTERTAINER.md** — short career high-income, domicile planning, image/likeness rights
- **MILITARY.md** — federal benefits, SGLI, DD-214, veteran services
- **ACADEMIC.md** — TIAA, 403(b), pensions, sabbatical planning
- **PUBLIC-EMPLOYEE.md** — defined-benefit pensions, deferred comp, survivor benefits

Each file covers:
- Compensation structures unique to profession
- Liability exposure and asset protection
- Retirement and benefits optimization
- Succession considerations
- Common mistakes specific to profession
- Integration with general estate plan

When a profession overlay is loaded, record that explicitly in
`analyses/plan-coverage-matrix.md`.
