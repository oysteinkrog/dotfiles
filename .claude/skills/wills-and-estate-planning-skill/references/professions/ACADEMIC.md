# Academic

Academic households often have specialized retirement plans, pensions, and cross-state or international mobility.

## Load This File When

- the user has university employment, TIAA / 403(b) assets, pension rights, or frequent institutional moves

## Special Planning Issues

- 403(b), 457, pension, and survivor options
- sabbatical or international appointment mobility
- royalties and book / lecture income
- university-linked benefits and beneficiary forms

## Documents To Collect

- retirement-plan statements
- pension election paperwork
- royalty and publishing agreements
- benefit beneficiary confirmations

## Common Mistakes

- ignoring pension survivor-option elections
- overlooking accumulated small accounts from multiple institutions
