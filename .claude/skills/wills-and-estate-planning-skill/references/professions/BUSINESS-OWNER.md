# Business Owner

For many owners, the business is the estate.

## Load This File When

- the user owns a closely held business or controlling interest

## Special Planning Issues

- succession vs sale
- key-person dependency
- buy-sell funding
- management continuity on incapacity
- unequal-child treatment when only some children work in the business

## Documents To Collect

- operating agreement / bylaws / shareholder agreement
- cap table
- buy-sell agreement
- insurance policies
- debt and guarantee schedules

## Common Mistakes

- leaving business equity equally to children with unequal involvement
- no backup manager or interim control path
- no liquidity to equalize nonbusiness heirs
