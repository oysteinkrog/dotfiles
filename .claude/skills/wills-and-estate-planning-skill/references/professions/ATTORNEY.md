# Attorney

Lawyers often have partnership economics, malpractice tail issues, client-confidentiality concerns, and fiduciary optics that differ from ordinary salaried workers.

## Load This File When

- the user is a lawyer or law-firm partner
- there is a book of business, partnership interest, or contingent-fee portfolio

## Special Planning Issues

- partnership or shareholder transfer restrictions
- tail malpractice coverage and unfinished-business issues
- trust/account or client-confidentiality handling
- succession for a solo practice

## Documents To Collect

- partnership / shareholder agreement
- malpractice policies and tail coverage details
- trust-account / operating-account authority structure
- buy-sell or retirement agreements

## Common Mistakes

- assuming the spouse or children can smoothly inherit an active practice
- ignoring client-file and trust-account obligations in incapacity planning
