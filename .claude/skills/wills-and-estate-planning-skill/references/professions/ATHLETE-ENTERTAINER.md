# Athlete or Entertainer

Short earning windows, publicity rights, and travel-driven domicile complexity change the plan.

## Load This File When

- the user has image / NIL / publicity rights, touring income, or compressed peak earning years

## Special Planning Issues

- domicile and residency complexity
- publicity / likeness rights
- spending support for extended family systems
- career-ending disability risk

## Documents To Collect

- endorsement and licensing agreements
- management contracts
- insurance policies
- entity documents for image / media income

## Common Mistakes

- no disability or liquidity protection despite earnings concentration
- no long-range plan for publicity and royalty rights
