# Business Continuity After Death or Incapacity

Use this file when the user materially owns, controls, or operates a business whose value
could collapse if nobody can act immediately.

Output to:

- `deliverables/business-continuity-activation.md`

---

## First 24 Hours Questions

1. Who can run payroll?
2. Who has authority over bank accounts and wires?
3. Who can speak to employees, vendors, and customers?
4. Who controls email, phones, and core software?
5. What agreements govern death, disability, or buyout?
6. What insurance exists for key person / buy-sell funding?

---

## Required Sections

1. emergency operators
2. bank and payroll access
3. customer / staff communication plan
4. governing agreements and trigger clauses
5. immediate legal and accounting contacts
6. actions for death vs incapacity

---

## Anti-Pattern

Do not leave a business owner with only a testamentary plan and no Monday-morning
operations plan.
